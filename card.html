Dog

A dog is known as "man's best friend."

  • Scientific Name: Canis lupus familiaris
  • Conservation Status: Domesticated
  • Number of Teeth: 42
  • Average Lifespan: 10-13 Years

Dogs have a strong relationship with humans that dates back to early hunters and wolves. Since their domestication dogs have served a number of roles in human society. They are a common pet, and can be found in many homes across the world.
